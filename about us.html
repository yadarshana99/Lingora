about Us

Our mission is to make learning English simple, fun, and accessible to everyone. We believe that mastering a new language shouldn't be stressful or expensive. That is why we provide a wide range of essential resources—covering everything from Grammar and Reading to Writing, Speaking, and Vocabulary—completely for free.

Whether you are preparing for your exams with our Model Papers or just looking to improve your daily conversation skills, Lingora is here to guide you every step of the way. Let's learn English together!
